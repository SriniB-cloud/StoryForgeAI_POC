US-003: Product Review and Rating

As a verified buyer on ShopFlow, I want to submit a star rating and written
review for a product I purchased so that my review is published on the product page.

Acceptance Criteria:
AC1: Only verified buyers can submit a review — unverified users see a disabled review form.
AC2: Star rating is required — submitting without a rating shows an error.
AC3: Review text must be between 10 and 500 characters.
AC4: Submitted review appears on the product page after moderation.
AC5: Each verified buyer can submit only one review per product.
