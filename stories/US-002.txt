US-002: User Registration

As a new visitor on ShopFlow, I want to register with my email and password
so that my account is created and I receive a welcome email.

Acceptance Criteria:
AC1: Registration form requires email, password and confirm password fields.
AC2: Email must be in valid format — invalid format shows an error message.
AC3: Password must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase and one number.
AC4: Confirm password must match password — mismatch shows an error message.
AC5: Submitting valid details creates the account and redirects to home page.
AC6: A welcome email is sent to the registered email address after successful registration.
